## 📁 Business Prompts

### Business Idea Validator
```
Analyze my business idea and provide honest feedback on:
1. Market viability (1-10)
2. Competitive landscape
3. Potential challenges
4. Recommended pivot or focus areas

Business idea: [YOUR IDEA HERE]

Provide a structured assessment with actionable next steps.
```

### Email Writer
```
Write a professional email for the following purpose:
- Purpose: [MEETING REQUEST / FOLLOW-UP / INTRODUCTION / THANK YOU / ETC]
- Tone: [FORMAL / CASUAL / FRIENDLY]
- Target audience: [AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]
- Key message: [MAIN POINT]
- Call to action: [WHAT DO YOU WANT THE RECIPIENT TO DO]

Include a compelling subject line and keep it under 200 words.
```

### Meeting Notes Summarizer
```
Summarize these meeting notes into:
1. Key decisions made
2. Action items with owners and deadlines
3. Open questions
4. Next steps

Meeting notes:
[PASTE NOTES HERE]

Format as a clean, actionable document.
```

Include a quote from a spokesperson and a boilerplate about the company.
```

```

3. Pricing
4. Marketing strategy
5. Market share estimate

Then provide opportunities for differentiation.
```

```

### Quarterly Goals Planner
```
Help me set quarterly goals for:
Quarter: [Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4]
Year: [YEAR]
Focus areas: [AREAS]

For each focus area, suggest:
1. 2-3 specific goals
2. Key metrics
3. Milestones
4. Potential obstacles

Make goals SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
```
