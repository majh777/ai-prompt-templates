# Business Opportunity Scan
**Date:** March 15, 2026  
**Source:** McKinsey Explainers, Market Trends Analysis

---

## Hot Sectors (2026)

### 1. Agentic AI
- AI that doesn't just chat but ACTS autonomously
- Reshaping workflows, decision-making, human-machine collaboration
- Gap: Enterprise automation tools, vertical-specific agents

### 2. Sovereign AI
- National/corporate control over AI stack becoming strategic priority
- Data sovereignty, local AI infrastructure
- Gap: Regional AI infrastructure providers, compliance tools

### 3. Wellness Economy
- Health span (not just lifespan) is the new focus
- Brain health + cognitive performance emerging as trillion-dollar market
- GLP-1 medications driving massive healthcare shift
- Gap: Preventive brain health, longevity diagnostics

### 4. Extended-Range EVs (EREVs)
- Solving EV range anxiety with hybrid approaches
- Bridge technology for full EV transition
- Gap: Battery tech, charging infrastructure in emerging markets

### 5. Micromobility
- Electric scooters, bikes for short urban trips
- Sustainability play + congestion reduction
- Gap: Last-mile delivery integration, fleet management

### 6. Sustainable Fuels & Hydrogen
- Green gasoline, hydrogen-based fuels
- Aviation + heavy transport hard-to-decarbonize sectors
- Gap: Production scaling, distribution infrastructure

### 7. RegTech
- Regulatory technology for financial services
- Compliance automation, risk management
- Gap: AI-powered compliance, cross-border regulation

### 8. Digital Twins
- Virtual replicas of physical systems
- Manufacturing, infrastructure, smart cities
- Gap: Affordable SMB solutions, integration platforms
